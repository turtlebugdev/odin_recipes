Chicken Quesadilla Chaffle

These are so quick and easy after a long day at work.

You'll need a couple things:

  • A chaffle-maker. You can order one off Amazon. They are basically single-serving waffle makers.
  • 1/3 cup cooked, chopped chicken
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup shredded cheddar jack cheese
  • 1/4 tsp Taco seasoning
  • Cooking Spray

Here's what to do.

  1. Heat up the chaffle-maker.
  2. In a small bowl mix the egg, taco seasoning.
  3. Once mixed add in the diced cooked chicken and the cheddar jack cheese.
  4. Add 1/2 of the batter into your mini waffle maker and cook for 4 minutes.
  5. If they are still a bit uncooked leave it cooking for another 2 minutes.
  6. Then cook the rest of the batter to make a second chaffle.
  7. Serve with salsa and/or sour cream for dipping.
